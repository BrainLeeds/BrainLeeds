Introducing BrainLeeds

The University of Leeds Neurology and Neurosurgery Society

Established in 2021, our aim is to foster a strong interest in the clinical neurosciences amongst medical students.

The field of neurology and neurosurgery is an ever-changing landscape filled with cutting-edge science.

BrainLeeds is at the forefront of delivering neurology and neuroscience to medical students in Leeds.
